ARTICLE INTELLIGENCE

Five independent evidence-based scorecards

Auditicle reports Technical SEO, GEO readiness, AEO readiness, RAG retrieval readiness, and AI citation readiness separately. A score can be final, provisional, or withheld based on evidence coverage.

Technical SEO

HTTP status, index controls, canonical identity, headings, metadata, structured data, performance, links, and rendering evidence.

GEO readiness

Observable provenance, source support, original contribution, article identity, and clear evidence presentation.

AEO readiness

Direct answers, definitions, lists, tables, question coverage, and answer-block extraction signals.

RAG retrieval

Section boundaries, context retention, descriptive headings, entity clarity, and retrieval-friendly passage structure.

AI citation readiness

Authorship, dates, stable identity, references, unique evidence, and crawler controls—without citation guarantees.

Rendered HTML comparison

Compare server-delivered HTML with the browser DOM and retain a server-HTML fallback when rendering is unavailable.
